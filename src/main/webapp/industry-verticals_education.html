Educational institutes are the foundation of society. In order to manage and maintain their delivery standards, it is important to understand challenges faced by them. With globalization in education field, the institutes face competition for recruiting and retaining students and Alumni Donors. Effective response mechanism requires real time accessibility of information without compromising fiscal and academic accountability.

Added to this, there is acute shortage of qualified teachers, which results in rising cost for staffing. To maintain delivery standards educational institutes need to manage individual school autonomy, yet maintain central control and manage faculty performance. Today’s virtual world makes it mandatory to provide means to every player in value chain a possibility of accessing information online.

Education vertical have a lot of tangible and intangible assets which need to be maintained in an effective manner.

Our solution and domain experts can study your business scenarios & business priorities, compare them with the Best Practices and suggest appropriate solutions which can bring about the following capabilities & value proposition:

  • Increased communication capabilities with multiple entities in value chain
  • Effective tracking of recruitment, registration and academic records for the students
  • Track and effectively manage faculty work load
  • Single data repository for enterprise wide information
  • Provide closer financial scrutiny and improved cash flow, lowering receivables and improving spend analysis
  • Provide robust profile information to personalize contents for communications and multiphase messaging campaigns
  • Maintain relevant, interactive dialogues with all constitutes
  • Provide accurate and consistent student record keeping
  • Provide self-service applications to empower staff and faculties
  • Increased interoperability with multiple solutions
