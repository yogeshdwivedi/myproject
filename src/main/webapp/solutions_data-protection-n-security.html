Data protection & Security
Your data is your most valuable asset, and Mgain Communication Services is dedicated to keeping your data secure 24/7/365. In today’s IT infrastructures, the data backup process has become increasingly complex, and the backup systems must match that complexity or your organization is at risk of losing its data due to faulty hardware, hackers, or viruses.

The experts at Mgainz understand the complexities of data protection, data security, and data recovery. We will work with you and your staff in an effort to understand your business requirements around RTO, RPO, and backup windows.

Data Protection (Data Backup)
We offer a variety of data protection services and data backup solutions, including:

  • Cloud backup – Your data is protected in the cloud using a data replication and compression method. Cloud backup from Mgainz ensures your data is protected with state-of-the-art encryption that only your company can access.
  • D2D (disk-to-disk) – In D2D backup, your workstations will copy your data from its primary hard drive to a secondary backup disk, allowing you to back up and restore data directly from the secondary drive.
  • D2T (disk-to-tape) – This data backup method protects your data by creating a copy directly from your hard drive to a magnetic tape. Typically found in organizations where archival stability is of upmost importance, D2T typically takes longer to restore but is a reliable solution. At Mgain Communication Services, we deploy the latest tape backup solutions available.
  • D2D2T (disk-to-disk-to-tape) – D2D2T combines a backup disk with tape backup to protect your data—giving you two backup copies of your most critical information.
  • Remote data vaulting – Providing stable data protection from computer crashes, Internet malware, and even theft or vandalism, remote data vaulting is simple and cost-effective. This data protection solution is compliant with many industry regulatory guidelines, including HIPAA, FINRA, SEC, and Homeland Security Regulations, and even protects against fire and flood.
