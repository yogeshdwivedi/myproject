OMNITURE Site Catalyst
Omniture was the Utah based company, Orem’s online marketing and web analytics business. Presently owned by Adobe Systems, Omniture is one of the most advanced and effective web analytics and online marketing tools. The company began its journey in 1996 and was founded by Josh James and John Pentana; it was also funded by Venture Capitalists and included some of the biggest names.

The company had a lot of products including SiteCatalyst which is a ‘Software as a service' or SaaS application service and web analytics service too and SearchCenter+ which assists with content network optimisation in the most popular search engines, as well as assists in paid search. Other than these, there is a bouquet of software products that include data warehousing, software testing, web content management, and server side analytics among other services.
