Retail
Today, with the growth of digital, mobile and social technologies, companies must create premium customer experiences to attract, retain, and grow customer relationships through many parallel sales channels – the impact of the omni-channel. Customer experience is the key to successful retailing in online and brick-n-mortar shopping, which can no longer be viewed as separate retail activities. To be successful, Brands and Retailers need to engage customers, provide services that drive interaction, and enable loyalty programs that retain and drive increased transaction volumes with market leading customer service.

Mgainz's Retail Solutions
To address the challenges and opportunities created by the evolving retail landscape, Mgainz has created a comprehensive suite of services and solutions that empower the Retail Eco-system and help the Brands or the Retails to gain a competitive advantage, increase customer conversion, reduce costs, and own their customer relationships. Mgainz offers Brands, Retailers and Retail-centric Software Companies (ISVs) tailored services and solutions to meet the needs of each element of the Retail Eco-system, including Digital Channel Enablement (online, mobile and social), Mobility (in retail, in store operations, in back office operations), and Platform Integration and Modernization (from legacy store management, merchandising, and point of sale systems to leading ecommerce platforms with mobile solutions and mobile payments). Our comprehensive solutions in each area of the Retail Eco-system are necessary for providing your customers a true omni-channel experience.

Ready to build your Retail Solution with us?
Mgainz brings a holistic and customized approach to problems that today’s retailers are facing. We understand evolving customer behaviors and the types of services and solutions retailers need to maximize their footprint in the retail marketplace. We stand ready to help retailers achieve their potential. Contact us today to explore how our custom solutions can drive your business success.
