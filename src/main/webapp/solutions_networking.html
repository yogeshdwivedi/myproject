Networking
IP Networking Solutions
Mgain Communication Services can help you create a more efficient and flexible IP networking infrastructure in order to meet your growing business needs at highly competitive costs. Our team of exceedingly skilled engineers can design the best IT environment for your company’s success with our networking services covering IWAN, Wireless, Instant Access, and Data Center Networking. We deliver best in class data center technologies that can be customized to fit your data center needs whether it be on-premises, virtual, hybrid or in the cloud. Mgain Communication Services specializes in the services below and looks forward to providing your company with a simplified IT networking plan, tailored to meet your company’s needs and growth.

IWAN
Low Cost WAN Without Compromise
As more and more applications are moving to Data Centers and the Cloud, the traditional WAN landscape is changing with it. Intelligent WAN (IWAN) enables you to leverage the internet path for cloud and internet access instead of your leased line without sacrificing reliability and security. Moreover, utilizing IWAN gives you the flexibility to choose any internet service provider you want without being tied down to your MPLS link.

Spend too much money on a leased link? Contact Mgain Communication Services today to see how our experienced team can help you cut your leased cost 35-65% by utilizing IWAN to increase bandwidth capacity and enhance traffic flows to protect application performance.

Wireless
Enterprise Mobility
Wireless technology is driving a shift to allow flexibility in the workplace. The workforce is increasingly demanding BYOD friendly environments as it enables them to use a device of their choosing while reducing corporate overhead cost for end user devices.

As your business needs evolve to accommodate a more flexible workforce, the dedicated, expert team at Mgain Communication Services can help you meet your business outcomes and implement customized wireless solutions. We can design, install and implement the best tailored wireless solution to fit your needs as we offer pre and post sales site surveys, along with full design and implementation into your network to ensure optimum wireless coverage.

Instant Access
Plug and Play Provisioning
Looking for ways to simplify your network? Mgain Communication Services’s dedicated team of experts can design networks with a single point of configuration and management across distribution and access layer switches leveraging Cisco Catalyst Instant Access. Instant Access saves your company both time and money by freeing up resources to focus on critical applications.

Data Center Networking
Data Center Technology & Virtualization
The role of data center technology has had a significant impact on the virtualization process for enterprise organizations over the past few years. Storing large amounts of data has never been easier, and data centers using virtualization technologies make it easier for large companies to simplify and scale their applications and workloads.

Mgain Communication Services provides data center technology and virtualization services throughout the United States. We provide custom-built solutions to maximize the efficiency of your applications and make doing business easier than ever before.

Data Center Technologies
We provide a variety of data center technologies to help your organization leverage key hardware to perform multiple roles. You no longer need to invest in redundant, ancillary components like additional data or storage switches simply to increase port count. We can design custom solutions to fit your data center needs whether it be on-premises, hybrid or in the cloud.

Our custom-built data center solutions streamline deployment time with dynamic provisioning.

Our solutions include:

  • Data center security – Technology has advanced faster than most businesses, leaving your organization in the dust when it comes to deploying a secure data infrastructure protected from hackers, viruses, malware and other threats to your information technology. We create a stable, secure environment for you to conduct business—all within the regulatory guidelines of any industry.
  • Storage networking – Mgainz engineers work with you to learn the ins-and-outs of your organization and create custom-build storage networks with superior performance, incredible stability, and the flexibility to scale efficiently.
  • Application networking – Mgainz has partnered with Cisco to create improved user experience and innovation to help you increase your ROI from your data center technologies with optimum application networking.
  • Data center automation and management – Data center management plays a crucial role in how you conduct business on a daily business. The better your technology performs, the faster you can get your products and services to market, making your organization more competitive than ever before. Mgainz engineers will completely automate your data center infrastructure to create an agile environment for enhanced performance.
  • Overlay Transport Virtualization (OTV) – Scaling Data Centers has never been easier and more efficient by leveraging OTV. OTV provides IP connectivity between remote data center sites at the customer’s edge without having to rely on an MPLS link, optimizing your bandwidth for optimal efficiency. Mgainz’s relationship with Cisco creates a seamless user experience to extend and interconnect your data centers.
  • Application Centric Infrastructure (ACI) – Accelerating application delivery while reducing cost for our customers is what drives the Mgainz team. Our engineers leverage Software Defined Networking (SDN) to provide a more agile, scalable and secure network infrastructure that improves visibility of applications using the network, bypassing vendor dependence.
